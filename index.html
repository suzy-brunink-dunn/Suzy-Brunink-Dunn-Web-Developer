Recipe Cards

These fun recipe cards represent an early static website project I completed through my online courses with Skillcrush. Here I practiced my basic CSS layout skills: using and clearing floats, applying the overflow property, and creating images with a static height and width.

Rogue Pickings

This single page site for a fictional food delivery service was created by pulling all design elements from a PSD file. I then pushed my HTML5 and CSS3 skills up a notch by applying flexbox properies and media queries to make the site fully responsive across mobile, tablet and desktop devices.

Cuppa Joe

I found the bones of this project through a course on building responsive websites and turned the original project into the fictional coffee shop of my dreams. Here I worked on using responsive CSS and media queries while also managing to give the site a little personality.

Unplugged

Unplugged, a fictional tech industry nature retreat, was converted from PSD files to create a mobile first responsive website. This site utilizes an HTML5 Boilerplate shell and features multiple pages, a form for user input, embedded iframe, and responsive typography.

About

A bit about me...

I'm a self-taught responsive developer and designer and I'm passionate about creating design solutions that are intuitive, enhance user experience, and tell the brand story in a fresh and compelling way. My story does not involve years of technical roles nor was I "born coding." I do bring with me years of professional experience, the ability to communicate effectively with creatives as well as the C-suite crowd, and the belief that a flexible project plan and thought-out workflow process can get you through just about anything.

In addition, a degree in theatre and advanced degree in Film Studies have not only honed my critial thinking skills but instilled in me a deep love of visual storytellling. As I transition my career into tech, I have found my love of web development and user design to be the perfect partner to my creative background.

Aside from obsessing over fonts, I can usually be found walking my precocious senior terrier, watching old movies, hikingbikingcanoeing, or sipping a fine old-timey cocktail on the rocks.
